Our Story

A culinary journey that began with a passion for great food and exceptional service.

Welcome to Test Delight

Established in 2010, Test Delight has been serving exquisite cuisine made from the finest locally-sourced ingredients.

What started as a small family-owned bistro has grown into one of the most beloved dining destinations in the city, thanks to our commitment to quality, innovation, and warm hospitality.

Our chefs combine traditional cooking techniques with modern culinary trends to create dishes that delight both the eyes and the palate.
